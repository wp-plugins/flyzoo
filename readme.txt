Start your 14-day free trial now!


* Multiple chat rooms: full featured group chats with avatars, emoticons, moderation, image and video with preview

* Live Support Chat with multiple operators: support chat requests are automatically routed to the first available operator

* Customize chat colors, features and greeting messages

* Eye-catching design

* Responsive chat on tablets and mobile devices

* Multiple in-browser, floating chat windows: browse your site and chat at the same time

* SSL Connection: automatic chat encryption for HTTPS/SSL sites

* Moderation: ban users and block IPs, clean chat rooms from forbidden words

* Chat and group chat history & transcripts

* Multilanguage

= Can I test the chat on my localhost? =

Yes, but you still need to provide your production url when you sign up.

= How do I go online and chat with visitors? =

1. Visit your website and locate the chat widget at the bottom of the page
2. Click on MENU > "Log in or Sign up" 
3. Enter the e-mail and password you used to join Flyzoo

The main button will turn from "Leave a message" to "We are online".

= How do I embed a chat room into a page? =

Log into the Dashboard, click on CHATS, locate your chatroom and click on "Get embedding code". 
Here you find the shortcode (or javascript version): copy the shortcode and past it into your preferred page.
(Please note: to embed a chat room you need at least the PRO plan)

= Will the chat slow down my site? =

No, chat is loaded asynchronously after the main content, our servers will handle all the chat workload.

= After removing the chat plugin, it still shows on the frontend, how do I remove it completely? =

Probably pages are being cached. Please try to:

- refresh your browser's cache
- if you are using a caching plugin please, force a refresh

this should fix the issue.

Otherwise, please check that you haven't embedded the chat script into some .php file (like footer.php for instance).
